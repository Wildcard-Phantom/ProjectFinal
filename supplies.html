Supplies: what you need for cats

Supplies is also a basic need for cats. Here is what the internet says what supplies you need for your cats

The main basics of supplies

  1. Litter box(Some will take large due to some cats liking big space)
  2. Bowls(For food and water) 
  3.  Scratching posts and pads
  4. beds 

Other items

  1. Brushes
  2. Shampoo
  3. Nail Clippers
  4. Cat Collars(Make sure to have a Phone number on it) 
  5. Toys and enrichment items
